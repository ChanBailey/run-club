Runners Inc

Welcome to Runners Inc!

We are a begginner friendly running group that is dedicated to becoming our best selves and helping support eachother towards this goal.

We run on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays at 4:30am - 6:00am

Each day we run at a different location which is communicated through our friendly Whatsapp chat and organised by our group leader Mick

We are based in Barbados!

We take full adavantage of the islands natural beauty and explore this through our runs.
